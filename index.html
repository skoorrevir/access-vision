About Us

Access Vision was established to address critical gaps in eye care for the uninsured and underinsured in Alabama. Operating on a lean, volunteer-driven model, we work independently to complement existing health services, ensuring that cost and distance are never barriers to sight.

Partnerships

We collaborate with local schools, churches, and nonprofits to host our mobile clinics directly where the need is greatest.

Our Mission

Mission: To improve health outcomes by providing accessible eye care.

Vision: A future where no individual experiences preventable vision loss due to lack of access.
